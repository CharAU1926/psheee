Why We Exist

Motivated by SDG 8 (Good Jobs) and SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption), we wanted to contribute to a more circular economy in our local community. Small actions make a real, positive impact.

The Problem

Why recycling is harder than it looks.

No Time

Many busy professionals simply don't have the time to sort and transport waste to recycling points.

Accessibility

Elderly and disabled individuals often struggle to carry heavy waste bags to collection bins.

Wasted Potential

Small items end up in general trash instead of being reused, polluting ecosystems and water sources.
